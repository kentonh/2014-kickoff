2014 Kickoff Mixer

2013 has seen a large boost in Wichita’s Creator Culture, and we’re going to celebrate what’s surely to be the next best year.

Join MakeICT, UpFront Wichita, Code & Coffee, and Startup Wichita for an evening of food, drink, and friends - new and old.

MakeICT will have homemade toys to play with like their Multitouch Table, 3D Printer, a CNC Foam Cutting Machine, and Drawbot. Hear about upcoming UpFront ICT, Code & Coffee events.

January 26th, 2014 at 6:00pm

@ The Labor Party
216 N. Mosley
